# Welcome to 7.Exchange

**7.Exchange routes every trade for the best _net_ result — across chains, bridges, and liquidity sources, from one non-custodial interface.**

Swap, bridge, and trade between networks in a single continuous flow, with no manual hops between tools. Most aggregators rank routes by their headline quote. 7.Exchange ranks by what actually reaches your wallet: the routing engine pulls quotes from every connected provider in real time, scores each path after gas, bridge fees, slippage, and MEV exposure, and executes the one that pays out the most. Your funds stay in your custody from start to finish.

One interface, several ways to trade:
