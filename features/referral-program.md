# Referral Program

7.Exchange offers a referral program that lets you earn rewards when users you refer execute swaps on the platform.

## How it works

1. **Sign in** Connect your wallet and sign in to access the affiliate dashboard.
2. **Create a referral link** Go to the Affiliate page and create a named referral link. You can create multiple links for different channels (e.g., one for Twitter, one for Discord, one for friends).
3. **Share your link** Send your link to others or share it publicly.
4. **Earn rewards** When someone uses your link and trades through a route that supports affiliate fees, 7.Exchange collects the provider affiliate fee to the platform fee wallet and records your eligible referral earning internally.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Stargate** routes aren't fee-eligible.
{% endhint %}

## Affiliate dashboard

Your affiliate dashboard shows:

- **My Links** All your referral links with performance data
- **Referral Activity** Real-time feed of trades made by your referrals, including swap details, reward amount, and status

## Creating referral links

When creating a link, you can:

- **Name it** Give each link a descriptive name so you can track where your referrals come from.
- **Bind wallets** Attach specific profile wallets to a referral link. This links the referral tracking to your wallet addresses.

You can create as many referral links as you need.

## Wallet bindings

Each referral link can be bound to one or more of your profile wallets. This determines which wallet addresses are associated with the referral relationship and where rewards are attributed.

To manage wallet bindings, open the actions menu on any referral link in your dashboard.

## Rewards

- Rewards are tracked in real time and visible in your dashboard.
- By default, referrers become eligible for **50% of the provider-reported affiliate or app fee** generated by qualifying referred swaps.
- Commission rates may vary and are set by 7.Exchange.
- Payouts are handled manually by an admin each week, not withdrawn directly from the dashboard.
- A referrer must have at least **$10 in unclaimed referral earnings** to be included in a payout batch.
- Balances below $10 remain unclaimed and carry forward until the referrer reaches the minimum.

## Leaderboard

Active affiliates can track their ranking on the **Referral Leaderboard**, which ranks affiliates by referral volume. A separate **Score Leaderboard** ranks all users by overall platform activity.

## Rules

- Self-referrals are not permitted. Creating referral links and trading through them with your own wallets violates the program terms.
- Fraudulent activity, fake accounts, or manipulation of referral metrics will result in suspension and forfeiture of earned rewards.
- 7.Exchange reserves the right to modify commission rates, program rules, and reward structures at any time.

For full legal terms, see [Terms & Conditions](https://7.exchange/terms).

{% hint style="info" %}
Referral rewards come from the platform's routing fee; they do not increase costs for the referred user.
{% endhint %}
